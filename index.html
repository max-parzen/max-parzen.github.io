Max Parzen

PhD Candidate - University of Edinburgh

Guiding Energy Storage Innovation with Energy System Modelling

#Open Science/Data Advocate #Let's waste less time


In my PhD research, I am guiding energy storage innovation with optimisation methods. It was found that cost minimising and profit maximising approaches fail to signal the future technology competitiveness. Hence, I am exploring the role of data-driven energy system models to assess the system-value of energy storage. Advanced tools and techniques are applied, such as open energy system models (PyPSA-Eur) and Machine Learning techniques.

I tapped with a Co-founding project in the food industry into the exciting world of start-ups. Even though I had to stop, the experience sparked some innovation projects such as VisViva! As a hobby, I am open to discuss or push your ideas/inventions to making them market-ready. Just drop me a message. I am happy to help! I like #AI #data #energy solutions.
